The Top TV Shows of 2021 and 2022

2022 Has been an exciting year for TV shows!! From the hit sensation that was squidgame, to new releases from Marvel including Moon Knight and Ms Marvel to new true crime shows that have griped thousands there truley is something for everyone and below we present our top picks on how to get absorbed in a brand new series.

Most Awaited New Seasons

  • Stranger Things- Season 4: The kids are growing up as the horrors continue in the penultimate season, with the show guaranteed to keep you on the edge of you seat- Available on Netflix.
  • The Boys- Season 3: The gore continues in this long awaited season that sees many more superheroes exploded... Available on Amozon Prime
  • Only Murders in the building- Season 2: If you want a more chilled viewing expereince then this might be the one for you with a fictional story about creating a true-crime podcast. Available on Disney Plus

New Arrivals

  • The Tindler Swindler: This documentary explores the nefaious world of the romance scam and is reminder that if something sounds to good to be true it probably is. Available on Netflix.
  • Squid Game: Drama takes a twist in this South Korean show that became an international phenomenon which sees struggling characters compete to the end for a cash prize. Available on Netflix.
  • Ms Marvel: A fresh addition to the Marvel Universe, this show introduces Ms Marvel as a young teenage superhero likely to appeal to younger audiences. Available on Disney Plus.
